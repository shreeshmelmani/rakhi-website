My Sweetest Dumbo 💖

On this Raksha Bandhan, I just want to say
you’re more than just a sister to me —
you’re my partner-in-crime, my cheerleader,
my forever emotional support.

Your smile makes my day brighter and your hug
is the warmest place in the world. I may not say it often,
but I’m really grateful to have you in my life.

You deserve all the happiness in the world and more.
I promise to always stand by you like you always do.
Love you to the moon and back, Dumbo 💫
